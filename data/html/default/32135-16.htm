Newbie Guide:
AOne-Handed Blunt Weaponhas a higher accuracy than other weapons, but a low Critical Rate. Blunt Weapons are designed to reliably cause a lot of damage. I recommend them for hunters, for example.
As you advance, you can acquire skills specifically for Blunt Weapons. Something to keep in mind about these weapons is that they have greader damage range than other weapon types. They may not be particularly attractive, but they are sturdy, reliable weapons.
